How we started
Built from frustration with the industry.

In 2019, our founding team — three prop traders and a software engineer — were paying for signal services that couldn't back up their claims. Win rates were fabricated. There was no explanation behind any trade. When a signal blew up, there was silence.

We started AlphaEdge with one rule: publish every trade, win or lose, with the reasoning in plain English. No hidden results. No paid testimonials. No bots posting fake wins.

Four years later, we have over 14,000 members across 60 countries, a verified 87% win rate over 90 days, and a community of traders who trust us because we've never given them a reason not to.

The signals are the product. The education is what turns subscribers into independent traders. That combination is what makes AlphaEdge different — and what we're still building toward every day.

We didn't build AlphaEdge to sell signals. We built it because independent traders deserve the same information and analysis that institutional desks have — explained, not just delivered.
— Marcus Reed, Co-founder & Lead Analyst

What we stand for
Our values.

These aren't just words on a page. They're the decisions we make every day about what to publish, what to say, and how to treat our members.

🔍
Radical transparency
Every signal is logged. Every result is published. We don't delete losing trades. Our full history is searchable and verified by independent auditors.
🎓
Education over dependency
We don't want subscribers who rely on us forever. Every signal comes with an explanation. Our goal is to make ourselves less necessary over time.
⚖️
Responsible risk
Every signal includes a stop loss and position sizing guidance. We talk about what can go wrong, not just what we expect to profit. Capital preservation comes first.
🤝
Community over competition
Our Telegram channels are spaces for learning, not noise. Members share setups, discuss analysis, and hold each other accountable. We moderate heavily to keep the quality high.
⚡
Speed without sloppiness
Every signal is reviewed by a senior analyst before it's published. We don't rush for the sake of being first. Accuracy beats speed, every time.
🌍
Global access
Institutional-grade analysis shouldn't require an institutional income. We price our service to be genuinely accessible, and we cover every major market timezone.
