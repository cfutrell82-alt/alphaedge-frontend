How it started
From a trading desk
to a platform

It started in 2021 when our founders — frustrated by the noise, bad signals, and pay-to-lose "gurus" flooding social media — decided to build something different. A platform where every signal is backed by data, every trade has a defined risk, and every member has access to the same tools the pros use.

What began as a private Telegram group of 12 traders has grown into a community of over 12,000 members across 40+ countries — all united by one goal: trading smarter, not harder.

Today AlphaEdge covers stocks, forex, crypto, and options — with real-time signals, educational resources, and 1-on-1 analyst access for Elite members.

2021 — Founded
Started as a private group of 12 traders sharing real-time setups on Telegram.
2022 — First 1,000 members
Launched the Pro tier and built our proprietary signal algorithm covering stocks and crypto.
2023 — Expanded to Forex & Options
Added 2 new market verticals and launched the Elite tier with 1-on-1 analyst sessions.
2024 — 10,000+ members
Crossed 10K active traders and launched the AlphaEdge web platform and dashboard.
2026 — AlphaEdge 2.0
Launched the full trading platform with wallet, scanner, and real-time signal dashboard.
