⚡ Signals
How do I read an AlphaEdge signal?▼
Each signal contains: Entry (the price to enter), Stop Loss (where to exit if wrong), Take Profit levels (TP1, TP2, TP3), and Rationale (why we're taking the trade). We recommend entering at or near the entry price, setting your stop loss immediately, and taking partial profits at TP1. See our Education Vault for a full walkthrough.
What markets do your signals cover?▼
We publish signals across four markets: US equities and ETFs (stocks), major forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, and more), cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, and major altcoins), and options (weekly plays on SPY, QQQ, and individual names). Free plan covers stocks and crypto only (delayed 4 hours). Pro and Elite cover all four markets in real-time.
How many signals do you publish per day?▼
We publish between 8–20 signals per day depending on market conditions. We don't force signals — if the market isn't giving us high-probability setups, we'll say so. Quality over quantity is the rule.
What is your verified win rate?▼
Our 90-day verified win rate is 87%. Every signal is logged — wins and losses — in our public signal history. You can view the full record from your dashboard. We never delete losing trades.
What should I do if a signal is already past the entry price?▼
If price has moved significantly past the entry price, do not chase. Skip the signal and wait for the next one. Entering late changes the risk/reward ratio in an unfavourable way. We publish enough signals that missing one is never worth chasing.
👤 Account & Login
How do I reset my password?▼
Click "Sign in" on any page, then click "Forgot password?" Enter your email address and we'll send a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires in 30 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support@alphaedge.com.
Can I change my email address?▼
Yes. Go to Dashboard → My Account → Personal Info and update your email address. You'll receive a verification email at the new address before the change takes effect.
How do I delete my account?▼
Go to Dashboard → My Account → scroll to the bottom. You'll find the account deletion option there. Deleting your account cancels any active subscription and removes your personal data in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by law.
💳 Billing & Plans
How do I upgrade from Pro to Elite?▼
Go to Dashboard → Billing → click "Upgrade to Elite". The price difference is prorated — you'll only be charged for the remaining days in your billing period. Upgrade takes effect immediately.
Can I cancel at any time?▼
Yes. Cancel anytime from Dashboard → Billing → Manage Subscription. You keep full access until your current billing period ends. We don't charge cancellation fees and we don't hide the cancel button.
Do you offer refunds?▼
Due to the nature of digital signal services, we generally do not offer refunds for subscription fees already charged. We may make exceptions for technical failures preventing access for 72+ consecutive hours. Contact support@alphaedge.com within 7 days.
Is my payment information secure?▼
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified processor. AlphaEdge never stores your card number, expiry date, or CVV. We only store a Stripe customer ID.
💬 Telegram Access
How do I join the Telegram channel?▼
After subscribing, go to your dashboard and click "Telegram Access" in the sidebar. Message @AlphaEdgeBot with /start to receive a 6-digit code, then paste it in the dashboard to link your account. You'll receive a private invite link automatically.
I didn't receive my Telegram invite link.▼
Make sure you've completed the 6-digit code step in the dashboard. If you've done this and still haven't received the link, contact support@alphaedge.com with your account email and Telegram username. We'll resolve it within 2 hours.
I was removed from the channel — why?▼
This is usually due to a subscription payment failure, cancellation, or a violation of our community rules. Check your billing status in the dashboard. If you believe it was a technical error, contact support with your Telegram username.
💰 Wallet & Tokens
What are Fortunex and Cloutium tokens?▼
Fortunex (FTX) and Cloutium (CLT) are AlphaEdge's exclusive tokens, currently in pre-launch phase. FTX is the platform utility token; CLT is the community reputation token. Subscribers can reserve an allocation in the Airdrops section. Both tokens require regulatory approval before official launch.
Which wallets are supported?▼
We support MetaMask and any WalletConnect-compatible wallet (Rainbow, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, etc.). Do not use an exchange deposit address to claim tokens — use a self-custodial wallet where you hold the private keys.
🔧 Technical Issues
I'm not receiving signal notifications.▼
First check that Telegram notifications are enabled for the AlphaEdge channel on your device (Settings → Notifications in Telegram). Also ensure you haven't muted the channel. If notifications are on and you're still missing signals, contact support with your Telegram username.
The dashboard isn't loading correctly.▼
Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R). Clear your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists, try a different browser. Check our platform status above for any known outages, then contact support if needed.
