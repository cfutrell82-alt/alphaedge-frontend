01

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use AlphaEdge:

CategoryWhat we collectWhy
Account info Name, email address, password (hashed) To create and manage your account
Subscription data Plan type, billing dates, subscription status To manage your subscription and access
Telegram info Telegram user ID and username (if you connect Telegram) To grant and manage channel access
Usage data Pages visited, features used, login timestamps To improve the Service and detect abuse
Device data IP address, browser type, operating system For security and analytics
Communications Support emails, feedback you send us To respond to your inquiries

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, CVV codes, or full payment card details. All payment processing is handled by Stripe (see Section 4).

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and subscription
  • Deliver signals and content through the Service and Telegram channels
  • Process payments and manage billing through Stripe
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, password resets, account notifications)
  • Send weekly performance recaps and service updates (you can opt out)
  • Improve and develop the Service based on usage patterns
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to your support requests

We will never use your data to train AI models, sell advertising, or share with data brokers.

03

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Stripe: Payment processing. Stripe receives your name, email, and payment details to process transactions. Stripe's privacy policy governs their use of this data.
  • Telegram: If you connect your Telegram account, your Telegram user ID is shared with the Telegram Bot API to manage your channel membership.
  • Email service provider: We use a transactional email provider to send receipts and account emails. They process your email address on our behalf.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics tools (e.g. Plausible or Fathom) that do not use cookies or collect personal data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of AlphaEdge or others.

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to use your data only as directed by us and to maintain appropriate security standards.

04

Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc., a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you enter payment information on our checkout page:

  • Your card details are entered directly into Stripe's secure elements and never touch our servers
  • We store only a Stripe Customer ID and subscription ID — never your card number, expiry, or CVV
  • Stripe may store a tokenized representation of your card for recurring billing purposes

For questions about how Stripe handles your payment data, see stripe.com/privacy.

05

Telegram & Third-Party Platforms

AlphaEdge delivers signals via Telegram, a third-party messaging platform. When you connect your Telegram account:

  • We collect your Telegram user ID to add you to the appropriate signal channel
  • Your activity within Telegram channels is subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy
  • We do not monitor your private Telegram messages
  • You can disconnect Telegram at any time from your account settings

AlphaEdge is not responsible for Telegram's data practices. We encourage you to review Telegram's privacy policy.

06

Cookies & Tracking

We use the following types of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Service to function (session management, authentication). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not track individuals across websites and do not set persistent cookies.
  • Preferences: Cookies that remember your settings (e.g. dashboard preferences).

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or sell data to ad networks. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings.

07

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for 12 months after deletion, unless legal obligations require longer retention
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements
  • Support communications: Retained for 2 years
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days

You may request earlier deletion of your data (see Your Rights below), subject to legal retention requirements.

08

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Passwords stored using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal data internally
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring
  • Payment data never stored on our servers (handled by Stripe)

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

09

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

10

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us at privacy@alphaedge.com.

11

International Data Transfers

AlphaEdge operates from the United States. If you are accessing the Service from outside the US, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, such transfers are made in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including through Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

12

GDPR & CCPA

For EEA/UK users (GDPR): Our lawful basis for processing your data is (a) contract performance — necessary to provide the Service you subscribed to, (b) legitimate interests — for security and improving the Service, and (c) consent — for marketing communications. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete your personal information, to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. To submit a CCPA request, contact privacy@alphaedge.com.
