Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 27, 2026 · Effective immediately

🔒 AlphaEdge takes your privacy seriously. We collect only what we need to provide the Service and never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

When you use AlphaEdge, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: First name, last name, email address, and hashed password when you register
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interactions with signals and the terminal
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe or NOWPayments — we do not store card numbers or crypto wallet keys
  • Telegram data: Your Telegram user ID if you connect your account to @AlphaEdgeProBot
  • Wallet data: Cryptocurrency balances and transaction history stored in our database
  • Device data: Browser type, IP address, and operating system for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the AlphaEdge Service
  • Send trade signal alerts via email and Telegram
  • Process subscription payments and crypto deposits
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Communicate important account and service updates
  • Analyze usage to improve our signals and platform

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on PostgreSQL databases hosted by Render.com in the United States. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Bcrypt password hashing — we never store plain text passwords
  • JWT tokens for secure session management
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Environment variable protection for API keys and secrets

4. Third-Party Services

AlphaEdge uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • NOWPayments — cryptocurrency payment processing
  • Render.com — hosting and database infrastructure
  • Telegram — signal delivery via @AlphaEdgeProBot
  • Binance — real-time market data for the trading terminal (public API, no personal data shared)
  • DexScreener — AEC Coin price data (public API)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To prevent fraud or protect the security of the Service
  • With service providers listed in Section 4 who process data on our behalf

6. Cookies & Tracking

AlphaEdge uses localStorage in your browser to store your authentication token and user preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@alphaedgetrading.site

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

AlphaEdge is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
