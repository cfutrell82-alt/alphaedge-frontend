Company overview

Short description (1 sentence)
AlphaEdge is a subscription-based trading signal platform that provides institutional-grade signals, live market analysis, and trading education across stocks, forex, cryptocurrency, and options to over 14,000 independent traders worldwide.
Full description (2 paragraphs)

AlphaEdge is a platform built by professional traders to give independent retail traders access to the same quality of analysis and signal infrastructure that institutional desks use. Founded in 2019 by a team of prop traders and engineers, AlphaEdge covers US equities, forex, cryptocurrency, and options markets, publishing every signal — win or loss — with full transparency and plain-English reasoning.

With over 14,200 subscribers across 60 countries, a verified 87% signal win rate over the past 90 days, and a 60+ course education vault, AlphaEdge is one of the most transparent and actively used signal platforms in the retail trading space. The platform also operates AlphaEdge Wallet, an on-site crypto wallet, and is in pre-launch for its native tokens Fortunex (FTX) and Cloutium (CLT).
