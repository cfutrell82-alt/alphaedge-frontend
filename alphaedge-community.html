How we keep it valuable
Community rules.

Our Telegram channels stay high-quality because we enforce these standards strictly. Violations result in immediate removal.

✅
Stay on topic
Keep discussion relevant to trading, markets, and AlphaEdge signals. Off-topic posts will be removed.
🚫
No promotion or spam
No promoting other signal services, courses, bots, or paid groups. Zero tolerance — immediate ban.
⚠️
No financial advice
Sharing setups and opinions is fine. Telling others to buy or sell specific instruments is not. We're a community, not a licensed advisory service.
🤝
Be respectful
Critique ideas, not people. Disagreements about trade setups are healthy. Personal attacks are not tolerated.
🔒
No sharing of signals outside the channel
Our signals are for subscribers only. Sharing in public groups, social media, or other services violates our Terms of Service.
