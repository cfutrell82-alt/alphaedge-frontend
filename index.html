What it does
Everything you need to trade smarter

SlickOptions is built for personal use — a clean, fast dashboard for watching markets and managing your own portfolio. No noise, no upsells, just your data.

📊
Live Market Terminal
Real-time charts across 60+ crypto, stock, and forex markets. Powered by live Binance WebSocket and Polygon.io data.
💼
Portfolio Tracker
Track your real positions and balances. See your total portfolio value update in real time as markets move.
🔌
Real Broker Connection
Connect your own Alpaca (stocks) or Coinbase/Kraken (crypto) account via API keys. Trades go directly to your own account.
🧪
Paper Trading
Practice strategies with $100K simulated paper money before committing real capital.
📓
Trade Journal
Log your own trade ideas, entries, exits, and notes. Track your personal performance over time.
🔒
Private by Design
Your data, your accounts, your trades. SlickOptions never holds funds or custodies assets on your behalf.
Connect your own broker

SlickOptions connects to your existing brokerage accounts using official APIs. You provide your own API keys — trades execute directly in your account, not through us. We never touch your money.
