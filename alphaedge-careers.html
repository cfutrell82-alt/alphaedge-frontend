Who we are
Our culture.

We're a small team that punches well above our weight. Here's what that actually looks like day to day.

🔍
Transparency is the default
We publish every signal win and loss publicly. That same honesty runs internally. We share financials with the whole team, flag mistakes openly, and don't spin bad news.
🚢
Ship fast, learn faster
We have 14,000 real users. That means feedback within hours of shipping. We move in small, fast cycles — weekly deploys, rapid iteration, no death-by-committee.
🧠
Everyone is a trader
Whether you're in engineering, design, or support — you'll be expected to understand our product from the inside. We cover education and provide access to all signals.
🤝
No politics, no hierarchy
We're a team of adults. You'll talk directly to founders. Ideas don't need to pass through three layers of management. Good work speaks for itself.
⚖️
Ownership over oversight
We hire people we trust and get out of their way. You'll own whole projects, not just tasks. We measure outcomes, not hours.
🌱
We invest in growth
Annual learning budget, access to industry events, and a dedicated growth conversation each quarter. We grow as AlphaEdge grows.

What to expect
Our hiring process.

We move fast and respect your time. Most roles are filled within 3 weeks of application.

1
Apply
Send your application. We read every one — no ATS black hole. Response within 3 business days.
2
Intro call
30-minute video call with your hiring manager. We talk about the role, the team, and your background.
3
Skills task
A focused take-home task — maximum 3 hours. We pay you for your time on any task over 2 hours.
4
Final interview
Meet the founders and one or two team members. Decision within 48 hours of the final interview.
